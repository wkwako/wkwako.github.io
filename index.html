about

I am a college graduate with a Bachelors of Arts in physics, with minors in both mathematics and philosophy. My biggest interests are astrophysics, and data analysis. During Summer 2018, I had the opportunity to work with Prof. Rick Watkins, conducting original research through analyzing cosmological data. I felt excited by the work and was inspired to pursue it further. I have a fascination with puzzles of all kinds: spacial, computational, philosophical, etc.

Recently, I have been preparing for graduate school in computer science by taking online classes and implementing various data structures and algorithms into personal projects for practice.

Outside of academics, I play video games and D&D. Sometimes I go outside.
